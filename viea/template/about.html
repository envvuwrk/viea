Founder and Managing Director

Akhil & Aiswarya, Fashion Designer couple, launched their Fashion organization, A Square London – The Fashion House, in 2013 in London. The couple did their Fashion studies in London and shortly started their Fashion Empire, A Square London - The Fashion House, in 2013, an UK registered Fashion organization

Akhil & Aiswarya have done numerous Fashion shows around London, UAE, and India. They are the first Fashion Designer couple to do an Ancient Mural Painting Fashion Show in London. The couple has also starred in a Fashion Reality show in Kerala, India for a national Television broadcaster in 2016.

A Square London – The Fashion House, launched the Vie Fashion Week in India with a star-studded line up of international Designers, Models, Celebrities, Beauty Queens and Media from around the globe in association with the Government Tourism department of Kerala, India

Vie International Awards (VIA) is the new International Award Night project by A Square London, in the Fashion and Life Style industry.
